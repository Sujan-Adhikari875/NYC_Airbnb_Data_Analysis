NYC Airbnb Analysis
1.	Peak Demand:
--	Reviews peak in June (month 6). So, increase prices and listing availability during peak months.

2.	Seasonal Trend:
--	From the analysis summer has highest reviews and Winter has lowest. Therefore, offer discounts and promotions in off-season to boost bookings.

3.	Room Type Demand:
--	Entire homes have lower availability compared to shared/ private rooms.
Focus on investing in entire home listings for better occupancy and revenue.

4.	Price Trend:
--	Prices depend on seasons. That’s why implement dynamic pricing strategy based on season.

5.	Location Demand:
--	Listings are concentrated in key areas like Manhattan and Brooklyn.  Focus investment and marketing on high-demand locations.

6.	Host Distribution:
--	Market is totally dominated by power hosts. However, encourage new hosts to balance supply.

7.	Review:
--	Review sharply increases in mid-year (June). So, make some plan for marketing campaigns before peak period.

8.	Availability:
--	Its significantly depends on the room types. Where supply-demand imbalance exists. So, adjust supply based on demand trends.

Conclusion:
“The NYC Airbnb market is seasonal and location-based, with peak demand in summer and higher occupancy for entire home, highlighting opportunities for dynamic pricing and targeted investment.”
